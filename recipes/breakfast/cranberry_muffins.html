Cranberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 C flour
  • ½ C sugar
  • 4 t baking powder
  • ½ t salt
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ C cooking oil
  • ½ C milk
  • 1 C whole cranberry sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, oil, and milk.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the cranberry sauce.
  6. Scoop the batter into muffin tins, filling each cup about ¾ full.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Brush the hot muffins with melted butter and sprinkle with sugar.
  9. Makes 12-20 muffins.
