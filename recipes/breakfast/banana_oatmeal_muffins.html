Banana Oatmeal Muffins

Ingredients X1 X3
Flour 4-1/2 C 4-1/2 C
Rolled Oats 3 C 3 C
Sugar ½ C 1-1/2 C
Baking Powder 2 t 6 t
Baking Soda 1 t 3 t
Salt ½ t 1-1/2 t
Eggs 2 6
Oil ¼ C ¾ C
Milk ¼ C ¾ C
Mashed Bananas 1 C (about 3) 3 C (about 9)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the oil, sugar, and eggs until well combined.
  3. Add the dry ingredients (flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda, and salt) and mix until well combined.
  4. Pour in the milk and mix until the batter is smooth.
  5. Fold in the mashed bananas.
  6. Scoop the batter into muffin tins, filling each cup about ¾ full.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool before serving. Enjoy!
