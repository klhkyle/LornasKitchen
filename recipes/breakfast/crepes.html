Crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ C flour
  • 2 1/4 C milk
  • 2 T sugar
  • ½ t salt
  • 2 T oil
  • 3 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 t vanilla

Instructions

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and make a well in the center.
  2. Pour in half of the milk and mix until the batter is smooth, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the eggs and mix thoroughly.
  4. Add the sugar, salt, oil, and vanilla, mixing until well combined.
  5. Add the remaining milk and mix until the batter is smooth.
  6. Let the batter sit for at least 1 hour.
  7. When you are ready to make the crepes, heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Pour about ¼ cup of the crepe batter into the pan and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the pan evenly.
  8. Cook the crepe for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to turn golden brown. Carefully flip the crepe over and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  9. Transfer the cooked crepe to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  10. Serve the crepes hot, with your choice of fillings and toppings. Enjoy!
