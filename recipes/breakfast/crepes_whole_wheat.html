Whole Wheat Crepes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2¼ cups milk
  • ¼ cup butter (or oil)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the eggs, salt, whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, milk, butter (or oil), and sugar.
  2. Blend the ingredients for about 1 minute, scraping down the sides of the blender as needed.
  3. Blend for an additional 15 seconds.
  4. Transfer the crepe batter to a covered container and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  5. When you are ready to make the crepes, heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Pour about ¼ cup of the crepe batter into the pan and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the pan evenly.
  6. Cook the crepe for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to turn golden brown. Carefully flip the crepe over and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  7. Transfer the cooked crepe to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the crepes hot, with your choice of fillings and toppings. Enjoy!
