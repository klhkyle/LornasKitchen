Breakfast Casserole

  • 24 oz. package of shredded hash browns
  • ½ C butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 C cream (half and half)
  • ½ t season salt
  • ¾ t dry mustard
  • ½ C onion, minced
  • 1 C shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1 C mild cheddar cheese
  • 1 C diced ham

Instructions:

  1. Thaw 24 oz. package of shredded hash browns.
  2. Spray a 9 X 13 inch pan and put hash browns in.
  3. Melt ½ C butter and pour over hash browns. Gently mix in butter.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
  5. While potatoes are baking, combine eggs, cream, season salt, dry mustard, onion, Swiss cheese, cheddar cheese, and diced ham in a bowl and mix together.
  6. Pour mixture over hash browns and mix gently.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.
