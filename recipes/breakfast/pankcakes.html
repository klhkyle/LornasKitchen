Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 ½ C flour
  • ½ C whole wheat flour
  • 4 T baking powder
  • 4 T sugar
  • 6 T oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the eggs, flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  2. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Pour in the oil and mix until the batter is smooth.
  4. Heat a nonstick griddle or pan over medium heat. Pour about ¼ cup of the pancake batter onto the griddle or pan, spreading it out into a circle.
  5. Cook the pancake for about 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles start to form on the surface and the edges start to turn golden brown. Carefully flip the pancake over and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Transfer the cooked pancake to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  7. Serve the pancakes hot, with your choice of toppings. Enjoy!
