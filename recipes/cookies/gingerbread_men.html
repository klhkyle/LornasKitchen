Gingerbread Men

Ingredients

  • ¾ C butter
  • 1 C sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 C dark molasses
  • ½ C sour cream
  • 5 C flour
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 t cinnamon
  • 1 t ginger
  • 2 t soda

Instructions

  1. Chill dough.
  2. Roll ¼ inch thick.
  3. Bake on an ungreased sheet at 375°F.
