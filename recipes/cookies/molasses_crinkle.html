Molasses Crinkles

Ingredients X1 X2
Margarine ¾ C 1 ½ C
Brown Sugar 1 C 2 C
Flour 2 ¼ C 4 ½ C
Baking Soda 2 t 4 t
Salt ¼ t ½ t
Cinnamon 1 t 2 t
Ginger 1 t 2 t
Egg 1 2
Molasses ¼ C ½ C

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the margarine, brown sugar, egg, and molasses until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and ginger.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined.
  5. Roll the dough into balls and roll in sugar.
  6. Place the balls on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, until the edges are slightly crispy.
