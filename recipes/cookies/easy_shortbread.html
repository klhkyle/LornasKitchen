Easy Shortbread

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. butter (margarine)
  • 2 C icing sugar
  • 1 C cornstarch
  • 6 C flour
  • almond and vanilla flavouring
  • salt (few grains)

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Roll in ball; flatten with a fork.
