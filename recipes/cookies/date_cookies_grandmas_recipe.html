Date Cookies (Grandma Smith's Rolled)

Ingredients

  • ½ C butter (or 1 C margarine)
  • ½ C lard
  • 1 C sugar
  • ½ C milk
  • 1 t salt
  • 3 t Baking Powder
  • 2 ½ C oatmeal
  • 2 ½ C flour (about)

Filling

  • 1 lb. Dates
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 1 C water
