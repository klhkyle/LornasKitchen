Rolo Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 1 c. firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 c. margarine or butter, softened
  • 2 tsps. Vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 c. chopped pecans
  • 48 Rolo® chewy caramels in milk chocolate
  • 1 tbsp. sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level off.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, cocoa, and baking soda; blend well.
  3. In a large bowl, beat 1 cup sugar, brown sugar, and margarine until light and fluffy.
  4. Add vanilla and eggs; beat well.
  5. Add flour mixture; blend well. Stir in ½ cup of the pecans.
  6. For each cookie, with floured hands, shape about 1 tablespoon dough around 1 caramel candy, covering completely.
  7. In a small bowl, combine remaining ½ cup pecans and 1 tbsp. sugar. Press one side of each ball into pecan mixture.
  8. Place, nut side up, 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
  9. Bake at 375°F for 7 to 10 minutes or until set and slightly cracked.
  10. Cool for 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheets. Cool completely on wire rack.
  11. Makes 4 dozen cookies.
