Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ C shortening
  • 2 ¼ C sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 t almond
  • 1 t lemon
  • 5 C flour
  • 2 t b.p.
  • 2 t salt

Instructions

  1. Cream shortening, sugar, eggs, and flavorings.
  2. Add dry ingredients and mix.
  3. Chill for 1 hour.
  4. Roll dough to ¼ inch thickness.
  5. Bake at 400°F for 4-6 minutes on greased pans.
