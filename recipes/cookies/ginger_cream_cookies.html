Ginger Cream Cookies

Ingredients

  • ¼ C soft shortening
  • ½ C sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ C molasses
  • 1 t. Soda dissolved in ½ C hot water
  • 2 C sifted flour
  • ½ t. salt
  • 1 t. ginger
  • ½ nutmeg
  • ½ t. cloves
  • ½ t. cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix together shortening and sugar.
  2. Add egg and molasses.
  3. Stir in soda dissolved in hot water.
  4. Sift together flour, salt, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, and cinnamon.
  5. Stir in sifted ingredients.
  6. Chill dough.
  7. Drop teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart. Grease sheet lightly.
  8. Bake at 400°F for 7-8 minutes.
