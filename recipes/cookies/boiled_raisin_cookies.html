Boiled Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 C raisins
  • 3 C water
  • 1 C shortening
  • 1 1/2 C sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • vanilla to taste
  • 3 1/2-4 C flour
  • 1 t cinnamon
  • 1/2 t cloves
  • 1 t soda
  • 1 t baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Boil raisins in water until 1 C water is left. Cool mixture slightly.
  2. Cream together shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add raisin mixture to the creamed mixture.
  4. Mix in flour, cinnamon, cloves, soda, and baking powder.
  5. Drop mixture by spoonfuls onto a cookie sheet.
  6. Bake cookies according to desired doneness.
