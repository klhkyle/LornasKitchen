Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 C shortening
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 C peanut butter
  • 3 C sifted flour
  • 2 t soda
  • ½ t salt

Instructions

  1. Form dough into balls.
  2. Press with a fork.
  3. Bake at 375°F for 10 minutes.
  4. Cool slightly.
  5. Yields 6 dozen.
