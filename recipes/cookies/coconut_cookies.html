Coconut-Oatmeal Crisp Cookies

When baking, allow plenty of space between these crispy cookies for spreading.

  • ¾ C margarine
  • 1 C sugar
  • ½ C brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 ¼ C coconut
  • 2 ½ C rolled oats

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together margarine, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Stir in coconut and oats.
  5. Drop by teaspoonfuls, 3 inches apart, on ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
  7. Cool 2 to 3 minutes on baking sheets; then remove to racks to cool completely.
  8. Makes 65 (2 ¼-inch) cookies.
