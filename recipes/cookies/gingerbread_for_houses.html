Gingerbread for Houses

Ingredients

  • ½ C shortening
  • ½ C sugar
  • ½ C dark molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 2 ½ C flour
  • ½ t b s
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 t ginger
  • 1 t cinnamon
  • 1 t cloves

Instructions

  1. Roll out dough on a lightly greased cookie sheet.
  2. Cut out house shapes with a knife in the dough on the cookie sheet, leaving excess to bake as well.
  3. Bake at 350°F for about 15 minutes.
  4. Draw over the shapes again and let gingerbread completely cool.
  5. When cool, remove them from the cookie sheet and let them dry out completely. It's good to let them dry overnight so they will be firm when you put the house together.
  6. Put the house together in stages, allowing each part to dry before the next stage. Start with the 4 walls and then add the roof.

Royal Icing (to glue the house together and to glue the candies on)

  • 1 egg white
  • flavouring (vanilla or almond)
