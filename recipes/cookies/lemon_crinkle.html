Lemon Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients

  • ½ C butter, softened
  • 1 C sugar
  • ½ t vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 t lemon zest
  • 1 T fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ t salt
  • ¼ t baking powder
  • 1/8 t baking soda
  • 1 ½ C flour
  • ½ C powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Grease baking sheets.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Whip in vanilla, egg, lemon zest, and juice.
  4. Combine all ingredients except powdered sugar.
  5. Roll 1 tablespoon of dough into a ball and roll into powdered sugar.
  6. Place on baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes.
  8. Let cool on pan for 3 minutes before removing.

Variation

Replace the lemon zest and juice with crushed candy canes. This variation should be baked on parchment paper to prevent sticking.
