No-Bake Cookies

Ingredients

  • ½ C milk
  • ½ C margarine
  • 4 T cocoa
  • 2 C sugar
  • 3 C rolled oats
  • 1 C nuts
  • 1 C cocanut

Instructions

  1. Bring milk, margarine, cocoa, and sugar to a full rolling boil.
  2. Pour over oats, cocoanut, and nuts.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Drop on wax paper.
