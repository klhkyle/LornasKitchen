Crazy Bean Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can green beans
  • 1 can yellow beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can Capuncino beans
  • 1 C chopped celery
  • 1 C chopped green pepper
  • 1 C Spanish onion

Marinade

  • ½ C salad oil
  • 1 t salt
  • ¾ C vinegar
  • ¾ C sugar

Directions

  1. Drain all the beans and set them aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the salad oil, salt, vinegar, and sugar to create the marinade.
  3. Add the beans, celery, green pepper, and onion to the marinade and mix well.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
