Honey-Lime Chicken and Apple Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken tenders, cubed
  • 2 apples, cored, peeled, and cubed
  • 1 (7oz) round of Gouda cheese, cubed
  • 1 (8 oz) package bowtie pasta, cooked and cooled
  • 1/2 cup cubed celery
  • 1 cup pineapple tidbits
  • 1 cup seedless red grapes, halved
  • 2/3 cup slivered almonds
  • Honey-lime dressing
  • Lemon twists and avocado slices for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in honey-lime dressing.
  3. Garnish with lemon twists and avocado slices.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Note: For best results, add dressing just before serving.
