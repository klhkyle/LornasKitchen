Greek Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz.) package angel hair pasta
  • 3 T Cavender’s Greek seasoning
  • ½ C olive oil
  • 4 ½ T mayonnaise
  • 4 ½ T lemon juice
  • 4 or 5 chopped green onions
  • 3 or 4 diced Roma tomatoes
  • 1 (4 ½ oz.) can sliced olives

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta and cool it.
  2. Mix the mayonnaise, lemon juice, and Greek seasoning together.
  3. Toss the dressing with the pasta.
  4. Stir in the green onions and let the pasta sit in the fridge for at least an hour. It tastes best if it's refrigerated overnight.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes just before serving and serve the olives on the side.
