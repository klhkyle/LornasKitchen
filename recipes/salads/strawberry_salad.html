Strawberry Salad

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Sliced fresh strawberries
  • Broken pecans
  • Red onion, chopped or thinly sliced

Directions:

  1. Combine the ingredients and serve with poppy seed dressing.

Poppy Seed Dressing:

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. dry mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp. onion flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup salad oil
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp. poppy seed

Directions:

  1. Combine sugar, mustard, salt, vinegar, and onion flakes. Beat in oil gradually (using mixer) until mixture is thick and smooth. Stir in poppy seeds.
  2. Cover and refrigerate.
