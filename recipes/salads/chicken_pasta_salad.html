Chicken Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz.) pkg. bow tie pasta
  • 1 (12 oz.) pkg. spiral pasta
  • 1 (20 oz.) can pineapple tidbits, drained
  • 1 C cashews
  • 1 C red or green grapes, cut in halves
  • 2 cans sliced water chestnuts, chopped and drained
  • 2 C finely chopped celery
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 pkg. craisins
  • 6 chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
  • 1 large can mandarin oranges, drained

Dressing

  • 1 (16 oz.) Kraft coleslaw mix (Purchase extra bottle and use if needed)
  • 1 C mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Cook the bow tie and spiral pasta according to package instructions. Drain the pasta well and set it aside to cool.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the cooked pasta, dressing, and green onions. Leave the mixture in the fridge overnight.
  3. The next day, add the pineapple tidbits, water chestnuts, celery, grapes, and chicken to the pasta mixture.
  4. Just before serving, add the cashews, mandarin oranges, and craisins to the pasta salad.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
