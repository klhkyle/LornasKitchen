Frog Eye Salad

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. acini di pepe pasta
  • Dash of salt
  • 2 T flour
  • 1 C sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 T lemon juice
  • 3 cans of drained mandarin oranges
  • 2 cans drained chunk pineapple
  • 1 can drained crushed pineapple
  • Miniature marshmallows
  • Tub of Cool Whip

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta for about 8 minutes in boiling water. Drain and rinse with cold water and drain again. Cool to room temperature.
  2. In a separate saucepan, mix together a dash of salt, 2 T flour, 1 C sugar, 2 eggs beaten, and the juice of 3 cans of pineapple. Cook until thickened. Cool until room temperature.
  3. Mix the cooled pasta with the thickened mixture.
  4. Add 3 cans of drained mandarin oranges, 2 cans drained chunk pineapple, and 1 can drained crushed pineapple.
  5. Stir in miniature marshmallows and a tub of Cool Whip.
  6. Serve and enjoy! This recipe serves 20 or more people.
