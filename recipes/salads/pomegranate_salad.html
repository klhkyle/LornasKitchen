Pomegranate Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head Chinese cabbage, shredded
  • 2 medium pomegranates, seeded
  • ¼ t coarse pepper
  • 1 C toasted slivered almonds
  • ½ head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 2 T hot chili sesame oil
  • 1/3 C rice vinegar
  • 2 T water
  • 1 T minced ginger
  • 2 T ketchup
  • ¼ C soy sauce
  • 1/3 C sugar
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • ½ t pepper
  • ½ C mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Combine the Chinese cabbage, pomegranates, coarse pepper, toasted almonds, and iceberg lettuce. Toss evenly and refrigerate.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the chili sesame oil, rice vinegar, water, minced ginger, ketchup, soy sauce, sugar, lemon juice, and pepper.
  3. Stir in the mayonnaise until the dressing is well combined.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to evenly coat.
