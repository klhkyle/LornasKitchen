Potato Salad Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 C milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ C sugar
  • 1 heaping T flour
  • ½ C vinegar
  • 1 t mustard (dry)
  • salt

Directions:

  1. Combine and whisk while heating until it comes to a boil.
  2. Boil for 1 or 2 min.
  3. Add 1 T of salad dressing.
