Cranberry Jello

Ingredients

  • 2 3 oz. cherry jello
  • 1 Â¼ C sugar
  • 2 C boiling water
  • 1 1/3 C pineapple juice
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • 2 C ground (chopped) fresh cranberries
  • 2 C crushed pineapple
  • 2/3 C chopped celery

Directions

  1. Dissolve jello and sugar in boiling water.
  2. Add pineapple juice and lemon juice. Mix well.
  3. Stir in cranberries, crushed pineapple, and celery.
  4. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch pan and refrigerate until set.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
