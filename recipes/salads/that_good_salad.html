“That Good Salad”

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • 2 cups tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese, grated
  • 2/3 cup slivered almonds
  • 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese (added after dressing)
  • Croutons (added after dressing)

Dressing:

  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cup oil

Directions:

  1. Blend the first four ingredients in a blender. Slowly add oil until the mixture is frothy.
