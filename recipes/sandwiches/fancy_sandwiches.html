Finger, Ribbon or Rolled Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 12 large sandwich loaves, sliced lengthwise (ask the bakery to do this and order 6 white and 6 brown loaves ahead of time)
  • A variety of fillings (see below for specific ingredients and instructions)

Egg Filling:

  • 4 dozen eggs, hard-boiled
  • Salad dressing
  • Seasonings

Ham or Roast Beef Filling:

  • 1 lb ham or roast beef, ground
  • Salad dressing
  • Ground sweet pickles
  • A little bit of pickle juice

Cream Cheese Filling:

  • 1 1/2 lbs cream cheese
  • Salad dressing
  • Well-drained chopped maraschino cherries (12 oz jar red, 6 oz jar green cherries)

Velveeta Filling:

  • 1 1/2 lbs Velveeta
  • Salad dressing
  • Dill slices or olives (about 4 per slice)

Shrimp Filling:

  • 3 tins of shrimp
  • Salad dressing

Salmon Filling:

  • 2 large tins of salmon
  • Chopped celery
  • Salad dressing
  • Seasoning
  • Well-drained dill spears

Directions:

  1. Mix the ingredients for each filling with salad dressing as indicated above.
  2. Spread the filling on bread and roll tightly in wax paper (refrigerate until ready to cut).
  3. Cut the sandwiches as desired.

Makes approximately 800 tiny sandwiches.
