Pineapple Nut Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • One 8-ounce package cream cheese
  • Milk or mayonnaise

Directions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the pineapple and sugar. Bring to a boil and cook until thick, stirring constantly. Cool.
  2. Stir in the nuts.
  3. Soften and mash the cheese with enough milk or mayonnaise to make a good spreading consistency.
  4. Combine the cheese mixture with the pineapple mixture. Spread on thin bread, top as a sandwich. Trim and cut into squares.

Makes: About 4 cups.
