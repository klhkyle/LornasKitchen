Chicken Croissants

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans canned chicken (the larger cans like you get at Costco)
  • 2 or 3 apples, cut into pieces (a firm sweet apple like Fugi apples)
  • Â½ onion (or a whole one- a sweet one, cut into small pieces)
  • Mayonnaise to taste and to hold it together
  • Pepper (1/2 tsp. Or even more)

Optional Ingredients:

  • 1-2 cups grapes (cut in half if large)

Directions:

  1. Cut croissants in halves and fill with the chicken mixture.

Note: You can also add grapes to the chicken mixture for added flavor. They should be cut in half if they are large.
