Almond Chicken Salad Sandwiches

Spread:

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves or 2 tablespoons dried basil

Filling:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cooked chicken meat
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Combine the filling ingredients.
  2. Spread each slice of bread with a thin layer of the butter mixture.
  3. Add the filling to half the bread slices and top with the other half, buttered sides in.
  4. Remove the crusts and cut the sandwiches as desired.

Makes 40 tea sandwiches.
