Egg Salad Tea Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • 8 hard-cooked eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh dill
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 20 slices best-quality white bread

Directions:

  1. Peel the eggs and place them into a medium bowl. Slice the eggs and then coarsely mash them with the back of a fork.
  2. Add the mayonnaise, salt, pepper, and dill to the eggs. Stir until well blended. NOTE: This mixture can be refrigerated, covered, up to two days.
  3. Spread the butter onto one side of each slice of bread. Spread the buttered side of 10 slices of bread with 2 tablespoons of the egg mixture. Top with the remaining slices of bread, buttered side down.
  4. Carefully cut the crusts from the sandwich with a sharp knife. Cut in half diagonally, then cut in half again.

Yields 10 whole sandwiches or 20 halves or 40 fourths.
