Wild Rice and Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (2/3 C) sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can condensed beef broth
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 C wild rice
  • 1 C long-grain rice
  • 2 T butter
  • 2 T snipped parsley

Directions:

  1. Drain the mushrooms, reserving the liquid.
  2. Mix the beef broth, reserved mushroom liquid, and enough water to make 2 cups.
  3. Add the onions and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the rinsed wild rice, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add the long-grain rice, return to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes longer.
  6. Add the mushrooms and butter, heat briefly, and add the parsley.
  7. Serve hot. Serves 6-8.
