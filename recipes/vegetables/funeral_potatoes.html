Funeral Potatoes or Cheesy Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 12 large potatoes or (32 oz.) bag of frozen shredded hash browns
  • 2 10 oz. cans cream of chicken soup
  • 2 C sour cream
  • 1 C grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 C butter, melted
  • 1/3 C chopped onion
  • 2 C crushed corn flakes
  • 2 T butter, melted

Directions:

  1. Peel the potatoes and boil for 30 minutes, until just tender. Cool and grate into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish (or put the hash browns into the baking dish).
  2. Combine the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, cheese, and 1/2 cup melted butter, and onions. Gently blend in the potatoes.
  3. Combine the crushed corn flakes and the 2 tablespoons melted butter. Sprinkle on top of the potato mixture.
  4. Bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot. Makes 12 servings.
