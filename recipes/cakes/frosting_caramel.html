Caramel Frosting (from Elsie - Aunt Helen's)

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C butter
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 1/4 C milk
  • 1 1/2 C icing sugar
  • vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Cook butter and brown sugar on low heat for 2 minutes.
  2. Add milk and boil.
  3. Remove from heat and add icing sugar and vanilla.
