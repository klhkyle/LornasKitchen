German Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 C oatmeal
  • 1/3 C cocoa (or 1/2 bar German chocolate)
  • 1/2 C margarine
  • 1 1/2 C boiling water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 C white sugar
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 C flour
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1 t soda
  • 1 t salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix together oatmeal, cocoa, and margarine. Let stand for 20 minutes.
  2. Add boiling water and mix well. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs, white sugar, and brown sugar together.
  4. Sift together flour, baking powder, soda, and salt. Add to egg mixture and mix well.
  5. Add oatmeal mixture and mix until well combined.
  6. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.

Frosting Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C margarine
  • 1/4 C brown sugar
  • 1/2 C white sugar
  • 1/4 C canned milk
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1/2 C nuts
  • 1 C coconut

Frosting Instructions:

  1. Bring margarine, brown sugar, white sugar, and canned milk to a boil.
