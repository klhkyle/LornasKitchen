White Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C shortening
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 3/4 C flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 C milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 beaten egg whites (stiff)

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all ingredients except egg whites.
  2. Fold in beaten egg whites.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30-35 minutes.
