Carrot-Pineapple Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 C flour
  • 2 C sugar
  • 2 t cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 t soda
  • 1 1/2 t salt
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1 (8 oz.) can crushed pineapple
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 1 1/2 C cooking oil
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 1/2 C chopped nuts
  • 2 C raw carrots, grated and loosely packed

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all dry ingredients.
  2. Drain pineapple; reserve syrup. Add pineapple syrup to dry mixture, add eggs, oil, and vanilla; beat 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in pineapple, nuts, and carrots.
  4. Bake in greased and floured 12 C Bundt pan at 325 for about 1 1/2 hours or until cake tests done.
  5. Cool 10-15 minutes; turn out on wire rack or serving plate to complete cooling.
