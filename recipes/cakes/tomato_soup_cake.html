Tomato Soup Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C butter
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1 1/2 C flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1 C raisins
  • nuts

Instructions:

  1. Cream together butter and sugar.
  2. Add soup and spices, then flour, raisins, and nuts.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
