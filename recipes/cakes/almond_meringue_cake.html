Almond Meringue Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 C margarine
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 t. vanilla
  • 1/2 t. salt
  • 1 1/2 t. baking powder
  • 1 1/2 C flour
  • 7 1/2 T milk
  • 6 egg whites
  • 1 1/2 C sugar
  • sliced almonds

Instructions:

  1. Cream margarine, add sugar, then egg yolks, and vanilla. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  2. Grease a 9X13 cake pan. Pour in batter.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip 6 egg whites. Add 1 1/2 C sugar and continue whipping.
  4. Spread meringue over cake batter and sprinkle with sliced almonds.
  5. Bake at 350.
  6. Serve with whip cream or ice cream and berries.
