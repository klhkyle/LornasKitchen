Chocolate Cake (All Fools)

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 C white flour
  • 1/2 C whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 C cocoa
  • 2 t soda
  • 2 t salt
  • 2 C sugar
  • 1 C sour milk or buttermilk (milk with 1 T vinegar)
  • 1 C oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 C boiling water
  • 1 t vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Stir together all ingredients with a mixer.
  2. Blend in 1 C boiling water and 1 t vanilla.
  3. Bake at 350 for 30-50 minutes.
