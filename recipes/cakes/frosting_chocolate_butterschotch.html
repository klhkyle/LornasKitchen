Chocolate Butterscotch Frosting

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. unsweetened baking chocolate
  • 1/2 C packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 C heavy whipping cream
  • 2 T butter
  • 1/4 t vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 C powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate with brown sugar and cream in saucepan on stove on medium heat, stirring often until smooth. Remove from heat and add butter, vanilla, and salt, stirring until butter has melted.
  2. With mixer on low speed, beat in 1 C powdered sugar. Increase the mixer speed to high and beat for 1 to 2 minutes or until fluffy. Reduce mixer speed to low and mix in remaining powdered sugar, then increase mixer speed and beat for 1 minute longer.
