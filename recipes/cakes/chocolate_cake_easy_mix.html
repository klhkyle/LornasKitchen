Chocolate Cake (Easy Mix)

Ingredients 8 inch square pan 9X13 pan
Oil 1/3 C 2/3 C
Bakers Chocolate 2 squares 4 squares
Water ¾ C 1 ½ C
Sugar 1 C 2 C
Eggs 1 2
Flour 1 ¼ C 2 ½ C
Slt ½ t 1 t
Baking Soda ½ t 1 t
Vanilla 1 t 2 t
Chocolate Chips 1 pkg. (6 oz.) 2 pkg. (12 oz.)
Nuts 1/3 C 2/3 C

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt together the oil and chocolate.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the melted chocolate mixture, water, sugar, egg, flour, salt, soda, and vanilla.
  4. Pour the batter into an 8 inch square pan or 9X13 inch pan.
  5. Sprinkle the chocolate chips and nuts on top.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
