Lemon Pudding Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3 TBS butter, melted
  • 1 tsp grated lemon peel
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 3 well-beaten egg yolks
  • 3 stiffly-beaten egg whites

Instructions:

  1. Combine sugar, a dash of salt, and flour. Stir in melted butter, lemon peel, and juice. Combine milk and egg yolks and add to lemon mixture.
  2. Fold in egg whites. Pour mixture into an 8x8 inch baking dish and place in a larger pan on the oven rack.
  3. Pour hot water into the larger pan until it is at least 1 inch deep. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 40 minutes.
