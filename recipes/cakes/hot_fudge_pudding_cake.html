Hot Fudge Pudding Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 C flour
  • 2 t baking powder
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 2 T cocoa
  • 1/2 C milk
  • 2 T shortening (melted or oil)
  • 1 C chopped nuts
  • 1 C brown sugar (packed)
  • 1/4 C cocoa
  • 1 3/4 C hot water
  • 1 t vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Blend flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, and 2 T cocoa in a bowl. Stir in milk and shortening. Blend in nuts. Spread in greased 9x9 pan.
  2. Sprinkle with mixture of brown sugar and 1/4 C cocoa.
  3. Pour hot water over entire batter.
  4. Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes. During baking, the cake mixture rises to the top and the chocolate sauce settles to the bottom.
  5. Serve warm, with vanilla ice cream if desired.
