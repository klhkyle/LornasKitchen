Frosting: Aunt Jane's Cooked Fudge Icing

Ingredients:

  • 2 C whipping cream
  • 4 1/2 C sugar
  • 5 Tbsp cocoa (1 Tbsp cocoa to 1 cup sugar)

Instructions:

  1. Cook on stove as you would for candy, until it reaches the soft ball stage (the cold water test).
  2. Do not scrape the sides of the pan or it will turn to sugar. If it does, add a bit of boiling water.
  3. Let it cool and then beat it by hand with a wooden spoon, with electric beaters, or with a drill with a beater stuck in it.
