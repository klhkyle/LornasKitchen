Christmas Fruit Cake (Wedding Cake)

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. butter
  • 2 C sugar
  • 12 eggs
  • 1 t soda
  • 2 t cinnamon
  • 2 t nutmeg
  • 4 C flour
  • 2/3 C molasses or corn syrup
  • 1 package puff raisins
  • 3 lb. raisins
  • 1 lb. walnuts
  • 1 lb. blanched almonds
  • 1 lb. candied pineapple
  • 1 pt. marichino cherries

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, add juice from the cherries, add soda to molasses, and beat until thick.
  2. Sift flour on fruit and add to cake batter.
  3. Line pans with foil and wax paper for baking.
  4. Bake at 275 for 3 hours.
  5. Place a pan of water in the oven while baking.
