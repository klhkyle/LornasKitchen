Lemon Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 C butter
  • 2 C sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 C lemon zest
  • 3 C flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 C lemon juice
  • 3/4 C buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Syrup Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C lemon juice
  • 1/2 C sugar

Glaze Ingredients:

  • 2 C powdered sugar
  • 3 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar together. Add eggs and lemon zest.
  2. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Combine lemon juice, buttermilk, and vanilla.
  3. Alternately add flour and buttermilk mixture to batter, beginning and ending with flour.
  4. Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes. Makes 2 loaves, 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2.
  5. Cool for 10 minutes.
  6. For the syrup, heat lemon juice and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Pour over warm cake.
  7. For the glaze, whisk together powdered sugar and lemon juice until smooth. Pour over top of cake and let it drizzle over the sides.
