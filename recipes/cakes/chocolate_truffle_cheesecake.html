Chocolate Truffle Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 C Oreo baking crumbs
  • 1/4 C melted butter
  • 3 pkg. (250 g each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 6 squares semi-sweet chocolate, melted and cooled
  • 3 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Combine crumbs and butter; press onto bottom of 9-inch spring form pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla on medium speed until well blended. Blend in melted chocolate.
  3. Add eggs one at a time; mix just until blended. Pour over crust.
  4. Bake at 350 for 45 to 50 minutes or until center is almost set.
  5. Cool completely, then refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight.
