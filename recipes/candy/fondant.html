Fondant (no Fail)

Ingredients:

  • 7 C icing sugar
  • 1 lb. butter
  • 1 can Eagle Brand Milk

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and Eagle Brand together. Slowly add sugar, beating until creamy. Mixture works best when stiff. Cover and cool in fridge.
  2. When cool, roll mixture into balls and dip into warm dipping chocolate.
  3. Hint: You can separate mixture into small batches to add a variety of flavors, such as chopped nuts or cherries.
  4. Hint: Coat hands in icing sugar before rolling mixture into balls. You may also wish to let balls cool down again in the fridge before dipping.
