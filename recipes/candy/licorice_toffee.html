Licorice Toffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 can eagle brand milk
  • 2 C sugar
  • 1 C butter
  • 1 1/2 C karo light syrup
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1 t anise or licorice oil
  • black food coloring

Instructions:

  1. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture reaches 234 or 240 degrees.
  2. Add anise or licorice oil and black food coloring.
  3. Pour mixture into a buttered 12 x 16 inch pan.
  4. Score mixture when cooled slightly.
