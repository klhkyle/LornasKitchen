Caramels

Ingredients:

  • 2 C brown sugar
  • 2 C white sugar
  • 1 C cream
  • 1 C butter
  • 1 C syrup
  • 1 C walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Boil ingredients to hard ball stage. Add nuts and pour into greased pan until cool.
  2. Cut into pieces.
  3. (For candy apples, omit the nuts and cook just under hard ball stage.)
