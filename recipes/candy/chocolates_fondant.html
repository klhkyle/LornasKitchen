Fondant

Ingredients:

  • 5 C sugar
  • 1/8 C corn syrup (about 2 T)
  • 1 3/4 C cream
  • 1/4 C butter

Instructions:

  1. Place the first three ingredients in a pot. Stir constantly until it boils. Do not stir after it boils and cook to soft ball stage.
  2. Pour mixture on the counter to cool. Beat until creamy.

Olympian Cream

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 3/4 C boiling water
  • 5 C sugar
  • 1/8 C corn syrup
  • 1 3/4 C cream
  • 1/4 C butter

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize 3/4 C sugar in a separate pan. Add the remaining ingredients.
  2. Follow the instructions for the Fondant recipe above.
