Stretch Candy

Ingredients:

  • 2 C sugar
  • 1/2 C water
  • 1 t glycerine
  • 2 1/2 T vinegar
  • 1 t flavoring

Instructions:

  1. Boil sugar, water, glycerine, vinegar to hard ball (260).
  2. Add flavoring.
  3. Pour mixture onto greased platter.
  4. When cooled, pick up mixture in hands and pull from hand to hand until candy is stiff. Stretch into a long rope. Mark 1 inch pieces with a knife. Break off pieces.
