Easter Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 C sugar
  • 1 C water
  • 2 pkg. gelatine
  • 2/3 C cold water
  • 3 egg whites
  • flavoring

Instructions:

  1. Cook 2 C sugar and 1 C water to hard ball stage.
  2. Dissolve 2 pkg. gelatine in 2/3 C cold water.
  3. Mix gelatine into sugar syrup while syrup is hot.
  4. Beat 3 egg whites until stiff.
  5. Add sugar syrup and gelatine mixture to beaten egg whites. Add flavoring and continue beating until thick.
  6. Fill in forms.
