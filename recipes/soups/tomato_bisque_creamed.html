Creamed Tomato Bisque

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 lb butter or margarine
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1 can (28 oz) tomatoes
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • 4 cups canned chicken broth
  • 2 cups half and half cream (or whole milk)
  • 1/2 tsp curry

Directions:

  1. Saute the celery, onion, and carrots in margarine or butter until softened.
  2. Add the flour and stir constantly.
  3. Add the tomatoes, sugar, basil, marjoram, and chicken broth. Stir well.
  4. Cover the pot and simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Puree 1/3 of the mixture at a time in a blender.
  6. Add the half and half cream (or whole milk) and curry. Do not boil the mixture once the cream has been added.
  7. Serve hot or cold. Serves about 8.
