Taco Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef, cooked
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 can whole tomatoes (blended up)
  • 2 cans corn
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 1 can (small) green chilies
  • 1 package taco seasoning

Toppings (optional):

  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, combine all of the ingredients and mix well.
  2. If the soup is getting too thick, you can add one of the cans of corn or beans without draining it.
  3. Let the soup simmer for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Top each bowl of soup with cheese and sour cream, if desired.
