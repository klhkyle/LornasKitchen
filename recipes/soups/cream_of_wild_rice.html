Cream of Wild Rice Soup

Ingredients:

  • 3 C wild rice, cooked (1 C raw rice yields 3-4 C cooked rice)
  • 1 lg. onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery, diced
  • 1 C ham, diced
  • 8 C chicken broth (or 8 C water and 8 chicken bouillon cubes)
  • 1 C ½ and ½ cream

Directions:

  1. Sauté the onion for 3 minutes or until softened slightly. Add 1 C flour a little at a time and do not brown.
  2. Add the broth to the mixture and stir until well blended. Add the rice and heat thoroughly.
  3. Add the cream to the soup, but do not boil.
