Lyssa's Tomato Bisque

Faun Turley 302.8678

Feb. 3, 2002

Ingredients:

  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cans (14.05 oz each) peeled tomatoes
  • 2 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried basil (or 2 T fresh basil)
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Sauté the onion and celery in a small amount of butter until they are clear, but not browned.
  2. Simmer all ingredients (except the chicken stock and sautéed onions and celery) in a pot for 15 minutes.
  3. Puree the mixture in a blender (in 2 batches) or use a stick blender until smooth.
  4. Melt 4 T butter, stir in 3 T flour, and cook for 30 seconds on medium heat. Whisk in 2 cups chicken stock.
  5. Stir the chicken stock mixture into the blended tomato mixture and season to taste. Simmer until slightly thickened (stirring constantly), about 5 minutes.
  6. Serve in a soup mug as is, or garnish with a spoonful of whipped heavy cream on top, bacon bits, and/or chopped parsley.
