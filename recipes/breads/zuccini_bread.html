Zucchini Bread

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 C sugar
  • 1 C oil
  • 2 C shredded zucchini
  • ½ C well drained pineapple
  • 3 C flour
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 ½ t cinnamon
  • 2 t soda
  • ½ t baking powder
  • ¾ t nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla until thick and foamy.
  2. Spoon in zucchini and pineapple.
  3. Add flour, salt, cinnamon, soda, baking powder, and nutmeg and mix until well combined.
  4. Pour mixture into a bundt pan or 2 loaf pans.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

For the orange glaze:

  • 2 C powdered sugar
  • 3 t heated milkr
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1/2 t orange extract
  1. Combine in a bowl and mix until smooth.
  2. Drizzle over cooled bread.
