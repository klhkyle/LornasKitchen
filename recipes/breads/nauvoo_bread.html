Nauvoo Bread

  • 5 C hot water
  • 2 T salt
  • ½ C potato flakes
  • ½ C oil or shortening
  • ½ C sugar
  • 2 T yeast
  • 1 C powdered milk
  • 10-14 C flour

Instructions:

  1. Combine hot water, shortening, potato flakes, powdered milk, and sugar. Stir to dissolve shortening. When temperature is tepid, add yeast.
  2. Mix in 5 C flour and the salt. Stir to a smooth sponge and elastic dough.
  3. Let rise until double, about 45 min., punch down and let rise again.
  4. Shape into loaves, let rise. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
