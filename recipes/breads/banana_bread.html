Banana Bread

Ingredients:

Ingredient x1 x2
Bananas 5 10
Eggs 4 8
Shortening 1 C 2 C
Sugar (white) 1 C 4 C
Sugar (brown) 1 C 4 C
Walnuts or chips 1 C 2 C
Flour 3 C 6 C
Soda 2 t 4 t
Salt 1 t 2 t

Instructions:

  1. Beat bananas to liquid, add eggs.
  2. Cream together shortening and sugar.
  3. Add banana-egg mixture.
  4. Sift together sifted flour, soda, and salt.
  5. Add walnuts.
  6. Pour into well-greased loaf pans.
  7. Bake at 250 for 1 1/2 hours.
