Ginger Marinated Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 C chicken broth
  • 1/4 C soya sauce
  • 1/4 C brown sugar
  • 3 T ketchup
  • 3 T grated ginger (or 3 t powder)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 T balsamic vinegar
  • 1 T olive oil
  • 1 lb pork tenderloin

Directions

  1. Whisk together the first 7 ingredients and marinate the pork tenderloin in the fridge for 2 hours.
  2. Remove the tenderloin from the fridge and set it out for 1 hour.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and brown the sides of the tenderloin (2-3 minutes).
  4. Bake the tenderloin in chicken broth and half of the marinade at 350Â°F for 30 minutes.
