Pizza Spread (Joanne Murphy's)

Appetizer or Snack

Ingredients

  • 8 oz package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 C sour cream
  • 1/4 C mayonnaise
  • 2 (4 oz) cans of salad shrimp, rinsed and drained
  • 1 C seafood cocktail sauce
  • 2 C mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 green pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tomato, seeded and chopped

Directions

  1. Mix the first 3 ingredients together. Spread over a 12' pizza pan.
  2. Spread the seafood sauce over the cream cheese mixture.
  3. Add layers of shrimp, green pepper, onions, and tomato. Cover with cheese.
  4. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
