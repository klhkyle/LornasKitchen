Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. sausage meat
  • 1 ½ lb. hamburger
  • 2 C oatmeal
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ C evaporated milk
  • 1 ½ t Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 t onion powder
  • 1 t celery salt
  • ½ t salt
  • ¼ t pepper
  • 1/8 t garlic

Sauce

  • 7/8 C vinegar
  • ¼ C lemon juice
  • 5 T cornstarch
  • 2 10 oz. cans consome soup
  • 1 ¼ C brown sugar
  • ½ C corn syrup

Directions

  1. Mix together the sausage meat, hamburger, oatmeal, eggs, evaporated milk, Worcestershire sauce, onion powder, celery salt, salt, pepper, and garlic.
  2. Roll the mixture into balls.
  3. Bake in a 350°F oven for 1 hour.
  4. To make the sauce, bring the vinegar, lemon juice, cornstarch, consome soup, brown sugar, and corn syrup to a boil. Stir until it thickens.
  5. Add the meatballs and simmer for ½ hour.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
