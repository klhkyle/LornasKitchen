Sloppy Joes

Ingredients

  • 1 C ketchup
  • 1 T brown sugar
  • 1 T lemon juice
  • Paprika
  • 2 t Worcestershire
  • 1 t mustard

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Serve the Sloppy Joe mixture over hamburger buns or rolls and enjoy!
