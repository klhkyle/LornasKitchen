Pork Café Rio Pork and Tomatillo Dressing

Café Rio Pork Ingredients

  • 3 lb pork roast
  • 2 C sugar (part brown, part white) (I only use brown)
  • 1 can Coke
  • 1-2 C salsa

Directions

  1. Slow cook the pork roast in a crock pot with a small amount of water added (if you buy the large pork from Costco, you can cook it in a slow oven with no water). Cook until the meat shreds easily, about 3 hours. Remove the meat from the crock pot and drain the liquid.
  2. Shred the meat and put it back in the crock pot. Add the sugar, Coke, and salsa, and stir to incorporate.
  3. Slow cook for an additional 1-2 hours. Serve in tortillas or on a salad.

Cilantro Lime Tomatillo

Ingredients

  • 1 package ranch dressing mix
  • 1 T Salsa Verde
  • 1/8 t Tobasco
  • 1/3 bunch cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3/4 C mayo
  • 3/4 C sour cream
  • 1/2 C buttermilk
  • 1 lime, zest and juice

Directions

  1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender until smooth.
