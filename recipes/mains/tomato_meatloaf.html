Tomato Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 ½ lb. hamburger
  • ½ C uncooked rolled oats
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¼ C chopped onion
  • 2 T chopped parsley
  • 1 T Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ t salt

Directions

  1. Combine ½ can of soup with the other ingredients; mix thoroughly.
  2. Shape the mixture firmly into a loaf pan.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
  4. Pour the remaining soup over the loaf; bake for 15 minutes longer.
  5. Serves 6.
