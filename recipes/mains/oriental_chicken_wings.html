Oriental Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. chicken wings

Sauce

  • 1 clove garlic
  • ¼ C soy sauce
  • 1 T lemon juice
  • 1/3 C corn syrup
  • ¼ t ginger

Directions

  1. Mix together all the sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Pour the sauce over the chicken wings in a baking dish.
  3. Bake in the oven at 350°F for 1 or 1 ½ hours, turning the wings often.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
