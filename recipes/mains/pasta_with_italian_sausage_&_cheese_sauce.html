Pasta with Italian Sausage and Cheese Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 T butter
  • ½ C chopped onion
  • 2 T flour
  • 2 C milk
  • 1 ½ C shredded Provolone or old Cheddar cheese
  • ½ lb. hot or sweet Italian sausage, coked and sliced
  • 2 T tomato paste
  • 1 T chopped fresh parsley
  • 15 ml cooked pasta

Directions

  1. Melt butter, sauté onion
  2. Blend in flour, stir in milk, cook and stir until it boils.
  3. Remove from heat, add cheese, tomato paste, parsley, stir until the cheese melts.
  4. Add cooked sausage and pasta to the cheese sauce, stir to combine.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
