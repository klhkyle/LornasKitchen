Chicken Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts (cooked and deboned)
  • 10 oz lasagna noodles
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 t poultry seasoning
  • 2/3 C milk
  • 1 pt sour cream
  • 1/2 lb grated Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 t salt
  • Ritz crackers
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Mix the chicken, cream cheese, soup, milk, sour cream, and seasoning. Simmer gently (do not boil).
  2. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cheese in a 9 x 13 inch pan.
  3. Use Ritz crackers and a bit of butter for the topping.
  4. Bake the lasagna at 375Â°F for 30 minutes.
