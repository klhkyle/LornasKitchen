Mozarella Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 6 pork chops
  • 1 C onion slices
  • 4 T flour
  • 1 C green and/or red pepper slices
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 t paprika
  • 4 T margarine
  • 1 1/2 C milk
  • 1 C shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 C grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Combine the flour, salt, and paprika, and use to coat the pork chops. Reserve the remaining flour mixture.
  2. Melt 2 T of margarine in a skillet and brown the pork chops. Transfer the chops to an 8x12" baking dish.
  3. Melt the remaining 2 T of margarine, add the onions and pepper, and cook until tender. Add the vegetables to the pork chops dish.
  4. Stir the remaining flour mixture into the fat in the pan. Add the milk and cook until the sauce is thickened.
  5. Combine the cheeses and add half to the sauce. Pour the sauce over the pork chops and vegetables.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F for 40 minutes or until the pork chops are tender. Top with the remaining cheese, then return to the oven until the cheese is melted or broil until lightly browned.
