Baked Salmon

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon pepper
  • Salmon pieces
  • Honey

Directions

  1. Mix the mayonnaise and lemon pepper together.
  2. Spread the mixture on the salmon pieces.
  3. Bake the salmon at 370Â°F for 30 minutes.
  4. Just before the salmon is finished baking, drizzle honey over it to glaze it.
