Tourtiere

Ingredients

  • 1 pastry dough recipe (I buy Robin Hood one)
  • ½ lb. ground veal
  • ½ lb. ground pork
  • ½ lb. ground beef
  • 1 onion, ground
  • ¼ C chicken broth
  • ¾ t salt
  • ¼ t pepper
  • 1/8 t clove
  • 1/8 t cinnamon
  • 1/8 t savory

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a pot and cook on medium heat until the meat is cooked without being dry.
  2. When the mixture is cold, put it in the pie crust and bake until it is golden. (You may want to brush milk on top crust that will make it brown.)
  3. Bake at 425°F for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy! Bon appétit!
