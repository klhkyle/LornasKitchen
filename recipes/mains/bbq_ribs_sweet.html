B.B. Barn Ribs

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs ribs
  • 1/4 t pepper
  • 1/4 t paprika
  • 1/4 t garlic powder
  • 1/4 t cinnamon
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 1/2 C applesauce
  • 1/4 C ketchup
  • 3 t lemon juice

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Marinate the ribs in the sauce in the fridge for 24 hours, turning in the sauce every 4 hours.
  3. Bake the ribs at 300Â°F for 1 1/2 hours, turning them while baking.
