Beef Chinese Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 1 large onion
  • 6 C finely chopped celery
  • 2 tins chou mein noodles
  • 1 tin mushrooms
  • 4 oz cashew nuts
  • 1 tin mushroom soup
  • 1 tin water
  • 2 T soy sauce

Directions

  1. Brown the hamburger and onion together in a pan.
  2. Boil the celery for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix together the chou mein noodles, mushrooms, cashew nuts, mushroom soup, water, and soy sauce in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Combine all the ingredients in the large mixing bowl and then put them in a large greased casserole dish (no lid).
  5. Bake at 350Â°F for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours. Serve with rice.
  6. Note: Add the noodles just before putting the casserole in the oven. Serves 10.
