Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 C elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 C butter
  • 1/4 C flour
  • 4 C milk
  • 1/2 C salt
  • 1 pound cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 t paprika
  • Dry mustard
  • (Optional: onion, ham, or peas)

Topping

  • 1/4 C butter
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers
  • Grated cheese

Directions

  1. Bake the macaroni and cheese at 45 minutes.
