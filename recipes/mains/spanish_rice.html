Spanish Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 T oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 green pepper
  • 1 C rice
  • 1 C water
  • 1 can tomatoes and green chilies
  • 2 t chili powder
  • 1 t salt

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan. Saut√© the onion and green pepper until they are soft.
  2. Add the rice and brown it in the pan.
  3. Add the water, tomatoes and green chilies, chili powder, and salt to the pan. Stir to combine.
  4. Cover the pan and simmer until the liquid is absorbed.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
