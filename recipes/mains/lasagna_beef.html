Beef Lasagna Wendy Layne's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 T chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 t salt
  • 1 large can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (13 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 package sliced mushrooms (or 2 cans) fresh is best
  • 1 large carton cottage cheese (creamed)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 t salt
  • 1/2 t pepper
  • 2 T chopped parsley
  • 1/2 C grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Brown the meat slowly in a large, deep Dutch frypan or pot, and drain off any fat. Mix the next 7 ingredients together in a bowl, then add to the meat. Simmer uncovered until thick (approx. 30 min), stirring occasionally.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling salted water according to the package directions (approx. 7 noodles).
  3. Make the cheese sauce by mixing together the cottage cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, parsley, and parmesan cheese.
  4. Grease a deep pan with butter. Place a layer of the meat sauce, noodles, cheese sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 50 minutes (covered with foil). Let sit for 10 minutes before serving.
