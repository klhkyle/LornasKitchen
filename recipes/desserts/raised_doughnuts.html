Raised Doughnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 C shortening
  • 2/3 C sugar
  • 2 t salt
  • 4 eggs well beaten
  • 3 C lukewarm water
  • 2 T yeast
  • 1 t sugar
  • 1/2 C water
  • 7-8 C flour (or more) - make a soft dough
  • 1/2 t nutmeg

Directions

  1. Cream the shortening, sugar, and salt.
  2. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in the water.
  3. Add the eggs, lukewarm water, and dissolved yeast to the shortening mixture and beat well with a mixer.
  4. Add the flour and nutmeg to make a soft dough.
  5. Let the dough rise, then knead it down. Let it rise again.
  6. Roll out the dough and cut it into doughnuts.
  7. Let the doughnuts rise again, then deep fry them.
  8. Dip the doughnuts in sugar, icing sugar, or whatever you prefer.
