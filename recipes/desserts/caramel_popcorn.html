Carmel Popcorn (Baked)

Ingredients

  • 1 C margarine
  • 2 C brown sugar
  • ½ C light syrup
  • 1 t salt
  • ½ t baking soda
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 6 quarts popped corn

Instructions

  1. Keep 6 quarts of popped corn hot in a slow oven (300°F).
  2. Melt margarine, stir in brown sugar, corn syrup, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  3. Boil for 5 minutes without stirring.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in baking soda and vanilla.
  5. Pour over popped corn and mix well.
  6. Turn into 2 large shallow baking pans.
  7. Bake in a 250°F oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
