Chocolate Hot Fudge

Ingredients

  • ½ C butter
  • 12 oz. can of evaporated milk
  • ½ squares unsweetened chocolate
  • 3 C powdered sugar
  • 1 ½ t vanilla

Instructions

  1. In a double boiler, melt butter and chocolate.
  2. Add milk and powdered sugar, stirring constantly on low until it boils.
  3. Boil for 8 minutes or until thick.
  4. Fold in vanilla and stir.
