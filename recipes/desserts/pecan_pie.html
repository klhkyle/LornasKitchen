Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup corn syrup
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup pecans
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup vegetable shortening
  • 1/4 - 1/3 cup ice water

Directions

  1. Make the pastry by mixing the flour, butter, and vegetable shortening together until crumbly. Add the ice water a little at a time until the dough comes together.
  2. Press the pastry into a pie dish and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar together until smooth. Add the corn syrup and eggs, mixing until well combined.
  4. Stir in the pecans.
  5. Pour the pecan mixture into the pie dish, spreading it evenly over the pastry.
  6. Bake the pie at 350Â°F for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown.
