Cookie Dough Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 C sugar
  • 1 ½ C flour
  • ½ C cocoa
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 C oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 t vanilla
  • ½ C chopped walnuts
  • ½ C butter or margarine
  • ½ C packed brown sugar
  • ¼ C sugar
  • 1 T milk
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 C flour
  • 1 cup (6 oz.) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 T shortening
  • ¾ C chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, cocoa, and salt. Add oil, eggs, and vanilla. Beat at medium speed for 3 minutes. Stir in walnuts. Pour into a greased 9x13 inch pan. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until brownies test done. Cool completely.
  2. For the filling, cream butter and sugars in mixing bowl. Add milk and vanilla; mix well. Beat in flour. Spread over the brownies; chill until firm.
  3. For the glaze, melt chocolate chips and shortening in a saucepan, stirring until smooth. Spread over filling. Immediately sprinkle with chopped nuts, pressing down slightly.
  4. Yield: 3 dozen.
