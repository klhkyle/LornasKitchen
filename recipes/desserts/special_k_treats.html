Special K Treats

Ingredients

  • 1/4 C brown sugar
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 1 C butter (or margarine)
  • 3 T vanilla
  • 3 C mini marshmallows
  • 6 C cereal
  • 1 C nuts (roasted, salted almonds or cashews)

Directions

  1. In a pan, melt the brown sugar, sugar, and butter.
  2. When the sugar has melted, stir in the vanilla and mini marshmallows.
  3. Pour the mixture over the cereal and nuts, and mix together.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto waxed paper, like you would for No Bake Cookies.
