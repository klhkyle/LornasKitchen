Jelly Roll

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 7/8 C sugar
  • 1 C pastry or cake flour
  • 2- ¼ t. b.p.
  • 1-½ T hot water
  • ¼ C sugar

Instructions

  1. Separate 5 eggs. Beat separated egg yolks and 1 whole egg until light.
  2. Add 7/8 C sugar and hot water. Stir in sifted flour and baking powder.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff and add ¼ C sugar.
  4. Fold egg whites into first mixture.
  5. Pour into a cookie sheet lined with wax paper. Bake at 375 for 12 minutes.
  6. Prepare a kitchen towel with wax paper sprinkled with icing sugar. Turn the baked cake onto the wax paper. Roll while it is still hot and let cool. When it is cool unroll and remove wax paper. Spread with lemon filling or your favourite jelly and roll up again.
