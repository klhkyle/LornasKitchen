Frozen Dessert

Ingredients

  • ½ C walnuts
  • 1 C flour
  • ¼ C brown sugar
  • ½ C butter
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 package frozen berries
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 C whipped cream (can be dream whip, canned milk or cool whip)

Instructions

  1. Mix the first 4 ingredients, bake in a slow oven. Cool.
  2. Beat egg whites and add sugar, frozen berries, and remaining ingredients.
  3. Fold in whipped cream.
  4. Spread ½ of the cooled crumbs on the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Spread the cream mixture on top, and then top with the remaining crumbs.
  5. Freeze.
