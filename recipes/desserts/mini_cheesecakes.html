Mini Cheesecakes

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 oz.) pkg. Cream cheese, softened at room temperature
  • 4 C powdered sugar
  • 1 pint of whipping cream, whipped
  • 1 (16 oz.) can cherry pie filling

Instructions

  1. Whip cream cheese and powdered sugar together till fluffy.
  2. Add whipped cream.

Directions

Put a spoonful of cream on each cookie and top with a cherry.
