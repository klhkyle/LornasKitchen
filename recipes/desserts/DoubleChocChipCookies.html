Double Chocolate Chip Cookies (Pam's Cookies)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, cocoa, soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto a greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 11.5 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool before serving. Enjoy!
