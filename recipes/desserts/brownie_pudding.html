Brownie Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 C sifted flour
  • 3/4 C sugar
  • 2 T cocoa
  • 2 t baking powder
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 1/2 C milk
  • 2 T salad oil
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 3/4 C chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 3/4 C brown sugar
  • 1/4 C cocoa
  • 1 3/4 C hot water

Instructions

  1. Stir together the first 5 ingredients.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  3. Stir in 3/4 C chopped walnuts (if desired).
  4. Pour into an 8x8x2 inch baking pan.
  5. Combine the brown sugar, cocoa, and hot water and pour over the batter.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F for about 45 minutes.
  7. Makes about 6-8 servigs. Serve with ice cream
