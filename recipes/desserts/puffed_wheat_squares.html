Puffed Wheat Squares

Ingredients

  • 1 C brown sugar
  • ½ C corn syrup
  • ¼ C butter
  • 2 T cocoa
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 7 C puffed wheat

Instructions

  1. Mix brown sugar, corn syrup, butter, cocoa, and vanilla in a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil and cook for no longer than 3 minutes.
  3. Pour mixture over puffed wheat.
  4. Pack mixture into a greased pan.
  5. Cool.
