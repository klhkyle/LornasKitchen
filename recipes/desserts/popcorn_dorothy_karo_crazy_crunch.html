Ingredients

  • 2 quarts popped corn
  • 1 1/2 C pecans
  • 2/3 C almonds
  • 1 1/3 C sugar
  • 1 C margarine
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1/2 C clear syrup

Directions

  1. Combine the sugar, margarine, and syrup in a saucepan.
  2. Boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Continue boiling and stirring for 10-15 minutes, until it reaches a light caramel color.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla.
  5. Pour the mixture over the nuts and popcorn, and break into pieces.
