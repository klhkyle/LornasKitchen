Tartar Sauce

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles
  • Lemon juice
  • Chopped onion
  • Lemon pepper
  • Sugar
  • Mrs. Dash

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix until well combined.
  2. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. Enjoy your tartar sauce with your favorite dishes!
