Antipasto

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. cauliflower
  • 2 lb. green pepper
  • 2 lb. red pepper
  • 2 lb. onions
  • 1 qt. dill pickles
  • 2 cans ripe olives
  • 2 cans green olives
  • 4 cans mushrooms
  • 4 cans tuna
  • 1 lg. can anchovies (not drained)
  • 2 cans French green beans
  • 100 oz. ketchup (6 ¼ C or 50 oz.)
  • 1 ½ C Mazola oil
  • 1 ½ C vinegar
  • 3 t salt

Directions

  1. Cut the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Boil the ketchup, oil, salt, vinegar, and peppers.
  3. Add the dills and onions.
  4. Boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the olives, tuna, mushrooms, and beans.
  6. Boil for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the cauliflower.
  8. Boil for 10 minutes.
  9. Put the mixture in jars and process for 10 minutes.
  10. Enjoy your antipasto!
