Bottled Beets

Ingredients

  • Beets
  • 1 t salt (per jar)
  • 1 T sugar (per jar) (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook the beets until the skins come off.
  2. Fill jars with beets and cover with the juice left from cooking.
  3. Add 1 t salt and 1 T sugar (optional) to each jar.
  4. Process the jars for 1 Â½ hours.
  5. Enjoy your bottled beets!
