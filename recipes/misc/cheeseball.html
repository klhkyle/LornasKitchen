Cheeseball

Ingredients

  • 2 - 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
  • 1 - 8 oz. wedge sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 T chopped pimento
  • 1 T chopped green pepper
  • 1 T chopped onion
  • 2 t Worcestershire sauce
  • dash of cayenne pepper
  • dash of salt
  • finely chopped pecans, to cover

Directions

  1. Combine the softened cream cheese and shredded cheddar cheese until well blended.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
  3. Chill the mixture.
  4. Shape the mixture into a ball and roll it in finely chopped pecans.
  5. Serve on crackers or on bread.
  6. Enjoy!
