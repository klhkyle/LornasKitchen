Pickled Beets

Ingredients

  • Cooked beets
  • ½ C vinegar
  • 1 t pickling spice
  • ½ C sugar

Directions

  1. Fill jars with cooked beets.
  2. Add the vinegar, pickling spice, and sugar to the jars.
  3. Fill the jars with the juice from the cooking beets.
  4. Process the jars for 1 ½ hours.
  5. Enjoy your pickled beets!
