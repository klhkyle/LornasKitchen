Wassail

Ingredients

  • 2 C water
  • 2/3 C brown sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 3 quarts apple juice
  • 1 quart cranberry juice

Directions

  1. Bring the water, brown sugar, cinnamon sticks, and cloves to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the spices.
  4. Add the apple juice and cranberry juice to the pot.
  5. Serve hot.
  6. Enjoy your wassail!
