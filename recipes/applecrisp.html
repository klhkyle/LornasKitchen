Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 4 C sliced apples
  • Sprinkle over apples:
    • ½ C sugar
    • ½ t cinnamon
    • 3 T butter
  • Topping:
    • ½ C flour
    • ½ C oatmeal
    • ½ C brown sugar
    • 1/ 8 t soda
    • 1/8 t b.p.
    • 1/8 t salt
    • ¼ C butter

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle the topping mixture over the apple mixture.
  2. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
